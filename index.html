About Us

We Help People In Need Around The World

THE WORLD HAS CHANGED, BUT CHILDREN'S NEEDS HAVE NOT. OUR COMMITMENT TO CHILDREN REMIANS AS STRONG AS EVER DESPITE THE COMPLEXITIES OF OUR WORLD.

Stephanie Arnold, CEO and Co-Founder

HOPEWELL’SFOUNDATION works in the world’s toughest places to reach the most disadvantaged children and adolescents – and to protect the rights of every child, everywhere. Across more than 20+ countries and territories, we do whatever it takes to help children survive, thrive and fulfill their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. we support child health and nutrition, safe water and sanitation, quality education and skill building, sickness prevention and treatment for mothers and babies, and the protection of children and adolescents from violence and exploitation. Before, during and after humanitarian emergencies, HOPEWELL’SFOUNDATION is on the ground, bringing lifesaving help and hope to children and families. Non-political and impartial, we are never neutral when it comes to defending children’s rights and safeguarding their lives and futures. And we never give up.
