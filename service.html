Child Education

 A lack of education leads to a continuous cycle of generational poverty. Our educational programs tackle this challenge head-on. By giving work to teachers and encouraging students to become doctors, teachers, dentists, business professionals and more, we can rebuild the community’s economy and give its people hope for a better future.

Medical Treatment

 Around 19 million children around the world are currently unvaccinated because proper medical care is unavailable in their country. Likewise, 2 million kids die every year from entirely preventable diseases — either because health care isn’t available in their community or due to a lack of funds to pay for treatment. We believe it is our God-given responsibility to end this suffering through medical volunteering and facility-building.

Donate Now!

Pure Drinking Water

 Clean drinking water and sanitization are fundamental human rights, and it can be hard to imagine anyone lacking such a simple need. In many third-world countries, children must bathe, drink and wash their hands from unclean surface water, which can be contaminated with waste. These conditions kill more than a million people every year. By constructing washrooms, drilling wells, investing in rainwater harvesting systems and providing hygiene items like soap, we can protect the health of millions of children.
